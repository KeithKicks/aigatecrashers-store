Frequently Asked Questions

What Midjourney version are these prompts optimized for?

These prompts are optimized for Midjourney v6+, which produces significantly more photorealistic results than earlier versions — especially for portraits and macro photography. They also work with v5. For the most convincing photorealistic output, we recommend v6. Version-specific notes are included in the prompts where the difference in output quality is most significant.

Can I use the generated images on my website or in my business?

Commercial use of Midjourney-generated images depends on your subscription tier. Pro and Mega plans allow commercial use; Basic plans have restrictions. Check Midjourney's current terms for your plan before using generated images commercially. Many customers use these images for blog posts, websites, social media, and product mockups — just verify your plan level covers your intended use case.

Do I need photography knowledge to get good results?

No photography knowledge is required. The prompts include technical camera terminology — aperture values like f/1.8 and f/8, focal lengths like 85mm and 24mm, lighting modifiers like softbox and rim light — because Midjourney responds to these descriptors to produce more realistic results. You don't need to understand what any of it means. Just paste the prompt as-is and get professional results.

Will people actually believe these are real photographs?

Many of them, yes. That's the benchmark we tested against when building this pack. The landscape, nature, and portrait prompts in particular produce results that customers regularly use on websites and social media without anyone questioning their authenticity. Results vary by generation — we recommend running 2-4 variations and selecting the strongest output. Portraits and action shots may occasionally show subtle tells, but the overall quality is consistently photographic.
