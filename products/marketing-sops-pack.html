Frequently Asked Questions

What format are these in?

Notion-based, fully editable. You get a master Notion page — duplicate it into your own workspace in one click and start customizing with your team's names, tools, and specific workflows. Notion is free for personal use, and the SOPs are designed so non-technical users can edit them without any training. Can I rebrand these for my agency?

Yes — replace the logo, update tool references to match your stack, add client-specific details, and use them with clients as part of your service delivery. Many agency owners present these SOPs to clients as evidence of their structured approach, which builds trust and reduces scope creep. The only restriction is that you cannot resell the raw SOPs as your own digital product.

Are these for B2B or B2C?

Both. The SOPs are organized by marketing function — not by industry, business model, or deal size. The procedure for running an email campaign, conducting a keyword research session, or setting up a paid ads campaign works the same whether you're selling enterprise software, professional services, e-commerce products, or local services. Customize the specifics to fit your context.

How detailed are they?

Each SOP includes: a stated purpose (what this procedure accomplishes), responsible roles (who owns each step), a numbered step-by-step procedure, a quality checklist (what "done right" looks like), and frequency or trigger information (when to run it). They're detailed enough to hand to a new team member on day one without additional explanation — that's the design standard every SOP was written to meet.
