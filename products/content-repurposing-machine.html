Frequently asked questions

Will the outputs all sound the same?

No — the chain is designed to voice-shift for each platform. The LinkedIn output reads like LinkedIn; the TikTok caption reads like TikTok. Same source idea, different voice.

What source content works best?

Blog posts over 800 words, podcast transcripts, and long YouTube videos. Short-form source (tweets, captions) doesn't give the chain enough to work with.

How long does one run take?

About 40-60 minutes end-to-end on your first try. 20-30 minutes once you've done it twice.

Do I need a paid AI subscription?

Recommended but not required. The chain runs on free tier of ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Paid plans give you more capacity for the 12-output batch.
