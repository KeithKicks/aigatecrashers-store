The system is designed around a 30-day path to first sale. Following the modules in sequence, most students who do the work hit their first transaction within that window. No. ChatGPT is one tool within a much larger system. This course covers market research, product strategy, funnel building, paid advertising, performance measurement, and scaling — a complete business curriculum. AI accelerates every step, but the system would work (just slower) without it.
