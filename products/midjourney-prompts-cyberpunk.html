What Midjourney version are these prompts optimized for?

Optimized for Midjourney v6 and later, which handles neon lighting and atmospheric depth exceptionally well — the glow falloff and reflective rain effects look dramatically better in v6. The prompts also work with v5. Where the version matters for a specific technique, we note it directly in the prompt parameters.

Can I use the generated images commercially?

Commercial use depends on your Midjourney subscription tier. Basic plans carry restrictions; Pro and Mega plans allow commercial licensing. Always check Midjourney's current terms for your specific plan. The prompts in this pack are yours to use freely with zero restrictions — no attribution, no licensing fees, no limits on how many times you run them.

Do I need art or design skills to get good results?

None at all. The cyberpunk aesthetic is highly specific — neon ratios, rain reflections, atmospheric haze, color temperature grading — and getting it right with a generic prompt is nearly impossible. These prompts encode all of that specificity so you get genuine cyberpunk output without needing to understand the craft. The parameters do the work.

Are these the same free cyberpunk prompts shared on social media?

No. Free prompts are typically one-liners that produce inconsistent results — sometimes great, usually mediocre, often nothing like what you wanted. Every prompt in this pack was tested across multiple generations, refined for consistent aesthetic coherence, and calibrated with specific parameters that make the difference between "kind of cyberpunk" and "exactly cyberpunk."
