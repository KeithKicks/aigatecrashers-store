Frequently asked questions

Is this HIPAA-safe?

The prompts themselves are HIPAA-neutral — you never put PHI into them. The kit includes a 1-pager on what to input and what to never input. For full HIPAA-compliant session notes, use a BAA-covered AI tool (not free ChatGPT). We tell you which tools have BAAs.

Does this work for therapists who don't take insurance?

Especially. Cash-pay practices need marketing more than insurance-only practices. Most of the kit is marketing-focused.

Can I customize the session-note templates?

Yes. The prompts build the skeleton; you always edit. Never paste AI-generated notes without review.

Is there a license for group practices?

Yes — the license is per-practice, unlimited staff. Pass it to associates.
