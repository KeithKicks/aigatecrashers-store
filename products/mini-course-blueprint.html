Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it actually take?

The system is designed for a weekend build. Some people finish in a day if they already have content they can repurpose. Others take a full weekend. It depends on how fast you write and how much source material you're starting with. The blueprint includes time estimates for each step so you can plan accordingly.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. The system works for text-based courses, slide decks, audio-only walkthroughs, or video — your choice entirely. Most first-time course creators choose slides or written guides because they're faster to produce and easier to update. Step 5 gives you production shortcuts for every format.

What platform should I host my course on?

The blueprint is completely platform-agnostic — it works with Notion, Teachable, Gumroad, Podia, Kajabi, or any platform you prefer. Step 5 includes a comparison of the most popular options based on audience size, pricing model, and technical complexity so you can choose the right fit for where you are right now.

What if my topic is too niche?

Niche is actually your competitive advantage. The more specific your topic, the easier it is to sell because buyers immediately recognize that you're speaking directly to them — not some generic audience. Step 1 includes a validation process to confirm your niche has a paying audience before you build anything.
