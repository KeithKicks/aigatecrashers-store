Common questions

The stuff people ask us every week.

Do I need to know how to code to use any of this?

No. Every product and training here is designed for non-technical owners and teams. You'll copy prompts, paste them into ChatGPT or Claude, and read the output. If you can send an email, you can use these.

Do I need a paid ChatGPT subscription?

No. The free tier of ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini works for most of what's in our prompt packs. A paid plan unlocks more capacity and newer models, but it's not required to get real results.

Is this safe for my non-profit? What about donor data?

Yes. We teach you which tools are safe for sensitive data (and which aren't), and every template includes a short "what to redact before you paste" checklist. Non-profits use these daily for grants, donor comms, and volunteer onboarding.

What if I've literally never used AI before?

Start with the free newsletter (50 starter prompts) or the ChatGPT Accelerator Challenge ($27), a 10-day walkthrough that takes you from zero to confident. Both are built for first-time users.

How does the refund policy work?

Every product has a 7-day, no-questions-asked refund. Email hello@aigatecrashers.com with your order number and we'll refund within 24 hours. We'd rather have the honest feedback than a grumpy customer.

Will this be obsolete in six months when a new model drops?

The specific prompts evolve, but the frameworks don't. We update the packs quarterly, and every update is free for people who already bought. The fundamentals of "how to talk to AI to get what you want" haven't changed since 2023.
